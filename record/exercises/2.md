## AIM

To build a Traffic Control System using Arduino Board.

## APPARATUS REQUIRED

- Arduino Uno
- Breadboards
- RTC DS3231 module
- I2C LCD Display (16x2)
- LEDs (4x Red, 4x Yellow, 4x Green)
- 220-ohm Resistors (x12)
- Pushbuttons (x3) (Menu, Up, Down)
- Jumper wires

## PRE-CONDITIONS

- The 12 LEDs (representing 4 directions: N, S, E, W) and their current-limiting resistors are wired correctly to the Arduino digital pins.
- RTC and I2C LCD are properly connected to the I2C pins.
- The required libraries (`Wire.h`, `RTClib.h`, `LiquidCrystal_I2C.h`) are installed in the Arduino IDE.
- The code is successfully compiled and uploaded to the Arduino Uno board.

## POST-CONDITIONS

- The traffic lights correctly sequence through Green, Yellow, and Red for North/South and East/West directions.
- The LCD displays the current time, status (Normal or Peak hours), and the active traffic phase.
- The timing dynamically adjusts based on peak hours retrieved from the RTC.
- The user can adjust timings (Peak Green, Normal Green, Yellow, Peak Hours) using the menu system.
