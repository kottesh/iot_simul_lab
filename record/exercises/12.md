## AIM

To simulate Smart Gas Leakage Detection System and Sending alert notification to the owner using MQTT Broker.

## APPARATUS REQUIRED

- Espressif ESP32 Dev Module
- Relay Module
- 2x LED
- MQ2 Gas Sensor
- OLED 1306
- Buzzer


## PRE-CONDITIONS

- Mosquitto MQTT broker running and reachable on the network.
- ESP32 flashed with Wi‑Fi credentials.
- MQ2 gas sensor connected correctly and calibrated (if calibration routine exists).
- Relay, buzzer and LEDs wired with proper driver/transistor if required.

## POST-CONDITIONS

- When gas level crosses threshold, system publishes alert over MQTT and activates buzzer/relay/LEDs.
- Owner receives alert notification (SMS or other configured alert method) or verify with serial/log and MQTT messages.
